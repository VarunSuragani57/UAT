National Outerwear MAP Holiday Program – 2014

 

Carhartt will offer a MAP Holiday Outerwear Order program to coincide with the Black Friday and Cyber Selling Week periods for 2014.  This will allow you, our partners, to play on a level playing field during this highly promotional selling period. All MAP Holiday Outerwear Orders must be placed through your sales rep.

Here are the parameters of the 2014 MAP Holiday Outerwear Order program:

1)      Accounts earn the right to participate in the MAP Holiday Outerwear Order Program by placing orders for the six Men’s Outerwear styles and one Women’s Outerwear style by 3/16/2014.

a.       •   J130   •   J140   •   J141   •   C26   •   C61   •   V26   •   WJ141  

b.      Order must ship between 7/1-10/31 and no revisions will be allowed

c.       Standard Fall ordering process rules apply and all orders must go through your sales rep

 

2)      Carhartt will supply the accurate number of units each account has earned by 4/15/14. 

a.       The discounted allotment is based off 20% of the initial order’s qualifying style units

                                                               i.      Example: Account has placed orders for a total of 100 units from the seven designated styles for shipment between the dates of 7/1/14 – 10/31/14. The account is now eligible to place a MAP Holiday Outerwear Order for 20 pieces of the designated outerwear (in any combinations of styles) at the proscribed discounted price.

                                                             ii.      Example’s math:  100 total units of seven designated styles x 20% = 20 units

b.      Your sales rep will provide your account’s accrued discount allotment

c.       The new, discounted order cannot exceed the total units indicated for your account

d.      MAP Holiday Outerwear Order(s) must go through your sales rep

e.      Normal billing terms and freight polices apply to MAP Holiday Outerwear Order(s)

 

3)      Accounts choosing to participate in the MAP Holiday Outerwear Program will be able to place a second, discounted order(s) for up to 20% of the original order’s qualifying style units.

a.       Again, only the qualifying MAP Holiday Outerwear styles apply to the discount order

b.      The new, Discounted MAP Holiday Outerwear Order is due by 5/15/2014

c.       The Discounted MAP Holiday Outerwear Order must ship between 11/1 -11/30

d.      MAP Holiday Outerwear Order must be placed by your sales rep

4)      Participating accounts may run MAP Holiday Outerwear Order pricing promotions 11/26-12/7. 

a.       All traditional media included: Radio-TV-Newspaper-Flyers and all On-line media

b.      MAP Holiday Outerwear Order pricing cannot be used in the media outside of 11/26-12/7

Key Dates to Remember:

3/16/14                Time to enter fall orders of qualifying products to earn discounted units
4/15/14                Date you will receive discounted units earned by your accounts
5/15/15                Cut-off date for discounted orders to be entered
11/1/14                Date shipping of MAP Holiday Outerwear Orders begins
11/26/14              Dates MAP Holiday Outerwear prices apply and may begin to be advertised
12/7/14                Date MAP Holiday Outerwear pricing program ends

12/8/14                Standard Fall 2014 pricing back in effect


FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS:

 

1.       ACCOUNTS MAY PLACE MULTIPLE MAP HOLIDAY OUTERWEAR ORDERS WITH MULTIPLE SHIP POINTS:

a.       Multiple MAP HOLIDAY OUTERWEAR ORDERS cannot exceed allotted units

b.       Multiple MAP HOLIDAY OUTERWEAR ORDERS ship points cannot exceed allotted units

 

2.       STANDARD FREIGHT POLOCIES ARE IN PLACE ON ALL MAP HOLIDAY OUTERWEAR ORDERS and ORDER(S) WILL BE VALUED ON THE DISCOUNTED PRICES …NOT THE REGULAR PRICE.

a.      Accounts that accrue 12 discounted units or less may add products outside selected styles to their MAP HOLIDAY OUTERWEAR ORDER to reach the prepaid freight threshold of $500.00.

b.       This exception applies only to the secondary MAP HOLIDAY OUTERWEAR ORDER

 

3.       ACCOUNTS DO NOT NEED TO PURCHASE ALL SEVEN (7) STYLES TO PARTICIPATE IN THE PROGRAM.

a.      Accounts may purchase any combination of discounted styles listed above/below.
